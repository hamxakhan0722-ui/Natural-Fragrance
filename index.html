Our Story

Founded by Hamza & Nisha

Hamza & Nisha, the founders of Natural Fragrance, started this journey with a simple belief: that everyone deserves to experience premium, long-lasting scents without compromising on affordability.

With a passion for luxury perfumery and years of research, they handpicked the finest imported oils to craft unique fragrances that truly stand out.
